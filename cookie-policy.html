1. Introduction

ASilva Innovations respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information through cookies and similar technologies.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, the types of cookies we use, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. This policy works in conjunction with our Privacy Policy which provides additional details about our data protection practices.

Our Commitment: We prioritize user privacy and provide meaningful choices regarding how your data is collected and used across our digital platforms, including our AI-powered resilience solutions and strategic planning platforms.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit our website or use our online services. They help us provide a better experience by remembering your preferences, understanding how our site is used, and enabling certain features.

What Cookies Can Do

  • Remember your login status and preferences
  • Understand how you navigate our website
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Analyze website performance and usage patterns
  • Display relevant advertisements based on your interests

What Cookies Cannot Do

  • Read data from your hard drive
  • Install viruses or malware
  • Access other cookies set by other websites
  • Identify you personally without additional data

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies based on different criteria to help you understand their purpose and whether you have a choice about accepting them.

By Lifespan

Cookie Type Description Typical Duration
Essential Necessary for the website to function properly. Cannot be disabled. Session or Persistent (up to 2 years)
Functional Enhances functionality and remembers your preferences. Persistent (typically 1-2 years)
Analytics Helps us understand how visitors interact with the website. Persistent (typically 6-26 months)
Marketing Used to track users across websites for advertising purposes. Persistent (varies by provider)

By Origin

  • First-Party Cookies: Set directly by ASilva Innovations. These help us improve our services and understand user behavior on our platforms.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external service providers (analytics, social media, advertising). These may track your activity across multiple websites.

5. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics, deliver advertisements, and more.

Why We Use Third-Party Services

  • Analytics Providers: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (Google Analytics, Hotjar)
  • Video Platforms: Enable embedded video content (YouTube, Vimeo)

  • Regular vendor security audits

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options to manage or control cookies on your device:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies: Prevent any cookies from being stored on your device
  • Accept all cookies: Allow all cookies without restriction
  • Notify you before accepting: Receive a warning before each cookie is stored
  • Delete existing cookies: Remove cookies already stored on your device
  • Select specific types: Choose which categories of cookies to accept

Browser-Specific Instructions

Browser How to Manage Cookies
Chrome Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
Firefox Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
Safari Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
Edge Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies
Opera Settings → Advanced → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Note About Functionality

If you choose to disable or reject certain types of cookies, some features of our website may not function properly. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for basic website operation.

7. Consent Management

We value your informed consent and provide easy ways to manage your cookie preferences at any time.

Initial Consent Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept All: Allow all non-essential cookies
  • Reject All: Decline all non-essential cookies (except essential)
  • Customize Preferences: Select specific cookie categories

Preference Center

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of any page on our website. This opens our Cookie Preference Center where you can adjust settings for:

Cookie Category Default Setting Affects
Essential Cookies Always Enabled Site functionality, security, and session management
Functional Cookies Enabled Personalization and enhanced user experience
Analytical Cookies Enabled Performance insights and usage statistics
Marketing Cookies Disabled Advertising and promotional content

Updating Preferences

To change your cookie preferences after the initial selection:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of any page on our website
  2. Click on "Cookie Settings" in the footer
  3. Adjust your preferences using the toggle switches
  4. Click "Save Preferences" to apply your changes
