Terms and Conditions

At ASilva Innovations, we provide cutting‑edge digital solutions with transparency and integrity. By accessing our website, products, or services, you agree to:

  • Use our platforms responsibly and lawfully.
  • Respect intellectual property rights and proprietary content.
  • Avoid misuse, unauthorized access, or harmful activities.
  • Accept that services may evolve, and updates may be introduced without prior notice.

We reserve the right to modify these terms to ensure compliance with industry standards and legal requirements.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. ASilva Innovations collects limited personal information to improve user experience and deliver tailored services.

  • Data Collection: We gather information such as name, email, and usage analytics.
  • Data Use: Information is used to enhance services, personalize content, and maintain security.
  • Data Protection: We employ encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls.
  • User Rights: You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time.

We never sell or share personal data with third parties without consent, except when required by law.

Cookies Policy

ASilva Innovations uses cookies to optimize performance and personalize your browsing experience.

  • Essential Cookies: Enable core functionality such as login and navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior and improve services.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings for a seamless experience.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be limited.

Accessibility Policy

We are committed to digital inclusivity. ASilva Innovations designs platforms that are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.

  • Compliance with WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards.
  • Support for screen readers, keyboard navigation, and alternative text for images.
  • Continuous testing and improvements to remove barriers.

Feedback on accessibility is welcome to help us improve inclusivity.

AI Ethics and Policy

As innovators in AI, we uphold ethical principles to ensure responsible technology use.

  • Transparency: Clear communication about how AI systems function.
  • Fairness: Avoiding bias in algorithms and promoting equitable outcomes.
  • Accountability: Human oversight in critical decision‑making processes.
  • Privacy: Protecting user data in AI applications.
  • Sustainability: Designing AI solutions that support long‑term societal and environmental well‑being.

We believe AI should empower people, not exploit them.
