Executive Profile

The Strategic Catalyst MIC Needs

A development management professional who re-engineers how institutions think about, measure, and act on risk — turning uncertainty into strategic advantage.

Alvin Silva brings nearly two decades of co-architecting resilience frameworks for global and national institutions. His philosophy treats risk management not as a defensive shield but as the engine of informed, courageous institutional action — precisely the mandate the Maharlika Investment Corporation requires as the Philippines' first sovereign wealth fund.

Currently co-architecting the Bangsamoro Investment Roadmap Development (BIRD) 2026–2035 — a 10-year framework attracting high-impact investments to complex post-conflict economic landscapes — Silva's work maps directly onto MIC's challenge of balancing national development with rigorous financial safeguards in a volatile global economy.
