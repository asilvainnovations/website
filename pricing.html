Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, checks, and government purchase orders. For LGUs, we work within GPPB procurement guidelines. Annual subscriptions can be paid quarterly for budget flexibility.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features. When downgrading, changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 30-day free trial of the Professional plan for qualified organizations. This includes full access to all features, onboarding support, and sample data to help you explore the platform.

What about data security and compliance?

All plans include enterprise-grade security with SSL encryption, regular backups, and compliance with Philippine Data Privacy Act. Enterprise and Custom plans include additional security certifications and audit logs.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs or non-profits?

Yes! Registered NGOs and non-profit organizations receive a 20% discount on all plans. Multi-year commitments receive an additional 10% discount. Contact our sales team for details.

How quickly can we get started?

Most organizations are up and running within 7-14 days. Starter and Professional plans can be activated immediately. Enterprise deployments typically take 2-4 weeks including customization and training.
