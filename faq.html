Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

What is ASilva Innovations?
ASilva Innovations is a Philippines-based technology company specializing in AI-powered resilience, compliance, and strategic planning solutions for LGUs, NGOs, SMEs, and social enterprises.

Who can benefit from ASilva Innovations’ solutions?
Our platforms support Local Government Units, NGOs, SMEs, educators, and researchers seeking adaptive, compliant, and sustainable strategies.

Solutions & Platforms

What is the DDRiVE-M Platform?
DDRiVE-M is our enterprise risk management system offering real-time risk indexing, compliance tracking, vulnerability mapping, and customizable dashboards.

What is Strategic Planner Pro?
Strategic Planner Pro is an AI-powered planning suite with SWOT analysis, balanced scorecards, portfolio tracking, and compliant plan generators.

What is Real-Time Leadership (RTL)?
RTL is a systems-based leadership framework (MOVE) that enhances situational awareness, ethical decision-making, and adaptive execution for leaders.

What is SPARC?
SPARC (Smart Predictive AI Resilience Calculator) is an AI-driven assessment tool that provides real-time risk and resilience insights.
