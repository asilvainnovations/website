Four-Part Framework

The MOVE Framework

Four pillars of mindful, decisive leadership designed for complex environments and rapid decision-making.

M

Master the Moment

Build rapid situational awareness using the "Mastery of Presence" element of the MOVE framework.

  • Building Rapid Context Brief
  • Stakeholder Lens Analysis
  • Triage Mapping to Classify Urgency vs. Impact

Recognizing Common Tunnel Vision Traps

O

Options Generation

Embrace creativity and divergent thinking. Involve diverse perspectives to avoid cognitive bias and discover innovative solutions.

  • Recognizing and Challenging False Binary Thinking
  • Use of Pathways Canvas to Structure Options
  • Pre-mortem Analysis technique

Expands solution possibilities

V

Validate Choices

Ensure robust decision-making through structured evaluation against values, impact potential, and feasibility constraints.

  • Decision Matrix (Pros/Cons)
  • Mentor feedback loops
  • Validation Compass framework

Reduces decision risk

E

Execute & Evaluate

Commit to decisive action and implement continuous monitoring. Adapt strategies in real-time based on outcome data and feedback.

  • Agile execution protocols
  • Outcome tracking systems
  • After-action review cycles

Drives continuous improvement
