Frequently Asked Questions

What are the 4 core solutions and what does each do?

DDRiVE-M is your end-to-end disaster resilience platform with 7 intelligence modules — from real-time hazard detection (PAGASA, PHIVOLCS, NOAH feeds) to AI-generated DRRM and recovery plans.

Strat Planner Pro is an AI-powered strategic planning tool that guides you from SWOT through systems thinking, to Balanced Scorecard, PAPs, and print-ready plan exports — and it works offline as a PWA.

Real-Time Leadership Center synthesizes data from all platforms into a live command dashboard for senior decision-makers, enabling agile response during high-stakes events.

AI Solutions Suite includes SPARC AI (predictive resilience calculator), DDRiVER AI chatbot, AI compliance plan generators, and sustainability AI aligned with SDGs and ESG reporting.

What does the 14-day free trial include?

You get full Professional-level access: DDRiVE-M (modules 1–5: Detection through Validation), Strat Planner Pro (all features including offline PWA), SPARC AI full resilience assessment, and DDRiVER AI chatbot. Up to 20 users, 50 GB storage, 24/7 support. No credit card required. Your data is preserved for 30 days after the trial ends.

Why is the Real-Time Leadership Center only in Enterprise and above?

The RTL Center aggregates live data across DDRiVE-M, Strat Planner Pro, and AI Solutions simultaneously — which requires multi-platform data pipelines, dedicated infrastructure, and role-based access controls suited for senior leadership teams. It's designed for organizations with complex, multi-department operations where a unified command view is mission-critical.

What does DDRiVE-M "Enhancement" module include?

The Enhancement module (available from Enterprise) uses AI to auto-generate DRRM plans, Business Continuity Plans (BCPs), and recovery roadmaps aligned with RA 10121, ISO 22301, and DILG standards. It includes resource allocation tools, recovery tracking, and templates based on the PSCP second edition — significantly reducing the time needed to produce compliance-ready documents.

What payment methods and procurement processes do you support?

We accept bank transfers, checks, and government purchase orders. For LGUs and national agencies, we work within GPPB procurement guidelines and can accommodate quarterly payments of annual subscriptions to align with government budget cycles. The Custom plan includes dedicated BAC process assistance for agencies undergoing public bidding.

What compliance standards does ASilva support?

DDRiVE-M is compliant with RA 10121 (Philippine DRRM Act), ISO 31000 (Risk Management), UNDRR MCR2030 (10 Essentials), and DILG MC 2020-161. The AI Solutions Suite additionally supports ISO 22301 (Business Continuity), GDPR, and Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173). Strat Planner Pro is designed for government MEL frameworks and national strategy alignment.

Can I change or upgrade my plan later?

Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with instant access to new solution modules. Downgrades apply at your next billing cycle. All your DDRiVE-M assessments, Strat Planner Pro plans, SPARC scores, and historical data are preserved across all plan changes.

Do you offer discounts for NGOs, non-profits, or multi-year plans?

Registered NGOs and non-profit organizations receive a 20% discount on all paid plans. Multi-year commitments (2+ years) receive an additional 10% discount on top of the NGO rate. Annual billing already saves 20% versus monthly. Contact our team to verify eligibility and stack applicable discounts.
